\begin{frame}{Hypothesis, not mechanism}
  \footnotesize
  \begin{itemize}
    \item Discussion idea: South China populations may appear relatively heat-adapted,
      while around \textasciitilde{}10\,$^\circ$C may still be consequential.
    \item This study cannot identify genes, ancestry effects or causal adaptation.
    \item Housing, air conditioning, behaviour, occupation, age, health care and
      acclimatisation remain alternative explanations.
    \item The 10\,$^\circ$C rule is a candidate exposure definition, not a biological cutoff.
    \item No stroke findings yet; synthetic outputs are not findings.
  \end{itemize}
